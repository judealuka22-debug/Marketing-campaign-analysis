# Marketing-campaign-analysis
I analyzed campaign performance using Power BI, cleaning the data and creating DAX measures to track key metrics. The dashboard revealed top-performing campaigns, audience engagement trends, and opportunities to improve ROI and guide data-driven marketing decisions.
OVERVIEW OF THE DASHBOARD
Household and Groceries are the top revenue and conversion drivers.
Highest revenue-generating products include Cold Drinks, Biscuits, and Dishwasher items.
Google Ads and Referral channels have the highest ad spend.
Household category received the most ad spend (17% higher than Beverages).
Peak performance occurred in December 2024 and January 2025 with the highest ROI and conversions.
February 2025 saw a major drop in ad spend, clicks, conversions, and ROI.
Strong impressions overall, but CTR can improve.
Opportunities exist to boost low-performing categories like Personal Care and Snacks through targeted promotions.
